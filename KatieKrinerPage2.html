Her Story

Mrs. Liptak’s story starts some time ago when she was a troubled teen that got mixed up in illegal hacking. Liptak became very good at hacking and became popular among the hacking community. However these were not the only ones who began to notice her. One day when she skipped school and went to the park, she was approached by a man. He gave her a contact card and told her that the FBI was interested in her abilities. She shrugged it off as a joke at first but curiosity got the best of her and she decided to contact the number on the card. To her surprise it was legit and she received a job offer in the FBI computer science division. Thus began her long and exciting FBI career.


During her career she helped to prevent many cyber attacks from taking place and helped the FBI to gather intelligence for ongoing cases. She quickly became widely known across many agencies and recieved many job offers from them. However, Liptak turned them all down because she enjoyed working for the FBI. She later began to go to many different places around country providing surveillance and technical assistance to agents who were in the field and became a highly regarded agent within the agency. Liptak eventually was tasked with her latest assignment which is being an undercover agent gathering intel on the youths of America as a teacher. She uses her FBI training to brainwash her students and make them become interested in computer science and web design to help supply the FBI with future prospects trained by the best.

My Story
